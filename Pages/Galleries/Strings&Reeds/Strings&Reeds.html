Strings and Reeds

Inpiration Explanation

Visiting a Catholic monastery with a friend during a chamber music recital, we wandered the grounds to find an extensive cienega behind the buildings. Soon after, in conversation with another friend, I learned she was taking cello lessons, played the violin and was willing to model using her instruments. We visited the monastery with permission to photograph on the grounds. She was very patient as I required her to trace her steps repeatedly so that I could acquire the shots for the panorama composite.
